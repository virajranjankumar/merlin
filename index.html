Effortless Monitoring

Laser guided AGV's deployed on warehouse and productions sites need continuous monitoring so that any potential issues are caught early.

Most newer AGV systems do not come with the tools and infustructure for long-term, multi-user/multi-AGV deployment scenarios. Our solution intends to fill that gap and provide the enterpise with the tools they need to monitor their automation investments.
